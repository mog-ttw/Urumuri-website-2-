# Urumuri Website — Presentation Script

**Estimated time:** 8–12 minutes  
**Format:** Live browser walkthrough  
**Start URL:** `http://localhost:8765/index.html` (run `./present.sh` first)

---

## Before you begin

> "Good [morning/afternoon], everyone. Today I'm going to walk you through the Urumuri website — our digital home for Rwanda's next-generation mobility ecosystem. Everything you're about to see runs in the browser: no app install needed for this demo."

---

## 1. Homepage — First impression (2 min)

**Page:** `index.html`

**Say:**

> "This is the Urumuri homepage. The name *Urumuri* means light — and our mission is to bring clarity, safety, and modern mobility to everyday life in Kigali and the surrounding districts."

**Point to the hero:**

> "Our headline — *Moving Rwanda Forward* — captures what we do. We're not just a ride-hailing app. Urumuri combines **transport**, **food delivery**, and **courier services** in one platform, built around people and powered by smart logistics."

**Point to the stats row:**

> "We operate **24/7**, deploy **200+ vehicles daily**, and we're already **trusted across Kigali and nearby districts**."

**Point to the right panel (animated cards):**

> "On the right you can see the ecosystem at a glance — electric rides, courier and food flow, and smart logistics working together."

**Scroll down slowly:**

> "As we scroll, you'll notice the cards animate in — we designed the site to feel human and lively, not like a generic template. The navy and gold colours reflect our premium, Rwandan-rooted brand."

**Point to the three service cards:**

> "These three cards are our core services:
> - **Urumuri Transport** — safe everyday rides with premium safety equipment
> - **Urumuri Food** — restaurant and grocery delivery for urban living
> - **Urumuri Courier** — professional package delivery with same-day options"

**Click:** `Urumuri Transport` card *(or come back to it in section 2)*

---

## Presentation tips

- Speak slowly while scrolling — let animations finish before you talk over them.
- Hover over a service card to show the playful icon wiggle and card lift.
- On mobile demo: open DevTools → toggle device toolbar → iPhone size → show hamburger menu.
- If something doesn't load, refresh once — the site has no backend dependencies.
- Keep the browser at 100% zoom and full-screen for a clean look.
